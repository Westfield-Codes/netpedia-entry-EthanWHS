What's Google Chrome?

Google Chrome is a web browser that was developed by Google, and released in 2008 for the Windows XP and newer. Google Chrome has evolved throughout the years, in 2009 a beta was released to support the platforms macOS and Linux (officially supported on May 25th, 2010). In February of 2012 Chrome launched a beta for Andriod devices, and it has since become the default browser
